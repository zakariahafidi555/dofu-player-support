Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Dofu Player?

You can organize videos, add custom headers, and stream from YouTube or other platforms. Visit the app settings for detailed instructions.

Can I add custom headers?

Yes, Dofu Player allows you to add custom HTTP headers for optimal streaming performance.

How can I save my favorite videos?

Simply click the "Favorite" icon while watching a video. Your favorites will appear in the dedicated section.

Troubleshooting

If you are experiencing issues with Dofu Player, try the following steps:

  • Restart the app and try again.
  • Check your internet connection.
